What I'm Working on Right Now

COVID-19 Therapeutic

I'm currently working with scientists from Novartis and student researchers from UCLA to develop an aptamer therapeutic for COVID-19. This would ultimately become more scalable than antibody therapeutics and more scalable than small molecule drugs. I'm using Gaussian Proccess models with bayesian optimization to optimize the normal HT-SELEX process, which often takes months.

MaculaX

My colleague and I recently founded a company called Macula Therapeutics, which aims to revolutionize the field of protein structure determination by innovating a faster and cheaper way to image proteins. You can learn more here

Technical Skills

I've done numerous projects at the intersection of machine learning and biology including CNNs for RNA splicing and DNA transcription factor binding models as well as LSTMS for small molecule drug generation.

Personal Growth and Values

One of my core beliefs is that everything that we do must help ourselves grow and stretch ourselves in some way. Here are the top three mindsets that I hold myself to.




Curiosity

The most important thing I ask myself before going to bed is 'what did I learn?' I devote entire days to piquing my curiosity in topics ranging from personal growth to innovations in biotech. I try to read 2-3 books per week.

Growth is the #1 Priority.

I treat every day as an experiment, changing one independent variable at a time, and seeing how that affects my productivity, creativity, and happiness levels. There's no point in living with the same practices and habits for every day of your life.

10X Thinking

I always aim to push myself beyond the limits of what I think is possible, both internally and externally. That means challenging my own perception of my ability and challenging the most traditional assumptions in the sciences.
